Background

I grew up on a native American reservation in northern California, in an isolated mountain household with extremely limited access to technology. As you might imagine, I was not one of those kids who was messing around in the command line at the age of 10. In fact, my family didn't even have a phone!

King's Quest 6, the only PC game I had growing up.

The first computer that I had access to could do two things as far as I could tell; it could run an MS Word and it could play King's Quest 6.
